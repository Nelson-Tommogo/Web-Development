How can blockchain be used in chemistry?

Chemical Supply Chain & Compliance

Blockchain can be used in End-to-end tracking.From raw materials to final product, blockchain can ensure that reagents, pharmaceuticals, or specialty chemicals are authentic and compliant with safety regulations. Blockchain can also be used in Anti-counterfeiting by linking blockchain to RFID or QR-tagged containers to help confirm the origin of high-value chemicals or APIs (Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients).
